DealerFinder is a fast, cache-first search tool for finding reliable car dealers across Germany — with smart filters, honest rankings, and a personal account to save your searches.

Why we built this

Finding a trustworthy car dealer in Germany is harder than it should be. Generic search engines return ads. Maps apps bury useful info under clutter. Review platforms are gamed.

DealerFinder cuts through that. We query Google Places, cache the results, apply real filters — rating confidence, working hours, contact availability — and show you the dealers that actually deserve your attention.

⚡
Cache-first speed
Results are cached for 24h. Most searches return instantly without hitting Google's API.
🧠
Confidence-adjusted ranking
A dealer with 4.6★ and 800 reviews ranks above one with 4.9★ and 3 reviews. As it should.
🔍
Real filters
Filter by radius, rating, open now, weekends, and contact availability — not just keywords.
